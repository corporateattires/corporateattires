About Us

Expertise

Corporate Attires has a decade's experience in the hospitality uniforms niche apparel market. The variety of departments and employee duties require special consideration when making your garment selections and implementing your hotel apparel program. Corporate Attires offers you expert guidance through the entire process. Our collection varies from wide variety of resort uniform shirts & classic suits for your front desk to heavy duty maintenance uniforms & wait staff apparel. 

Consultation

Our targeted consultation will help focus your needs, budget and time and your specialist will present you with smart options that work for the needs of your particular hotel property. Creating a cohesive uniform program for your entire hotel staff, from the front desk uniforms and bellman jackets to restaurant aprons and security shirts, is our business and our passion.
